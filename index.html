What We Do

Ithaca Study Spot Finder helps you find study spaces that match your needs and preferences. Whether you need a quiet place for focused reading, a social environment with background noise, or a late-night spot to finish work, this site makes it easier to compare options before you go.

You can browse study spaces, learn about each location's environment and amenities, and use the site to decide which space best fits the kind of work you need to do.
