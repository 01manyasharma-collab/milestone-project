Quiet

1. Kroch Library

Kroch Library is one of the quietest study spaces in Ithaca, making it ideal for focused reading, writing, and long solo study sessions.

  • Noise: Low
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours:
    • Sunday–Thursday: 8 AM to 12 AM
    • Friday–Saturday: 8 AM to 6 PM

2. Law School Library

The Law Library offers a calm and structured study environment that works well for reading-heavy classes and memorization.

  • Noise: Low
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Limited
  • Hours:
    • Mon–Thurs: 8 AM to 8 PM
    • Friday: 8 AM to 5 PM
    • Saturday: 11 AM to 5 PM
    • Sunday: 12 PM to 8 PM

3. Tompkins County Public Library

Tompkins County Public Library is a quieter off-campus option for students who want a more relaxed public study space downtown.

  • Noise: Low
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours:
    • Monday: 8 AM to 8 PM
    • Tuesday–Thursday: 8 AM to 8 PM
    • Friday: 8 AM to 5 PM
    • Saturday: 11 AM to 5 PM
    • Sunday: 12 PM to 8 PM

Social

1. Collegetown Bagels (Collegetown)

CTB has a lively atmosphere with steady background noise, which makes it a good choice for lighter studying or working with friends.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Some
  • Hours: 6:30 AM to 12 AM

2. Le Café

Le Café offers a comfortable study environment with drinks, seating, and a more relaxed social atmosphere than a library.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours:
    • Monday–Thursday: 8 AM to 8 PM
    • Friday: 8 AM to 5 PM
    • Saturday: 11 AM to 5 PM
    • Sunday: 12 PM to 8 PM

3. Bank Coffee

Bank Coffee is a good option for students who want a café setting that still feels productive and easy to work in for a few hours.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Some
  • Hours:
    • Monday–Thursday: 7 AM to 7 PM
    • Friday–Saturday: 7 AM to 9 PM
    • Sunday: 7 AM to 7 PM

Group

1. Mann Library

Mann Library has tables and collaborative areas that work well for small group meetings, shared studying, and project work.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Seating: Group tables
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours:
    • Monday–Thursday: 8 AM to 10 PM
    • Friday: 8 AM to 6 PM
    • Saturday: 12 PM to 6 PM
    • Sunday: 12 PM to 10 PM

2. Duffield Hall

Duffield Hall is a strong option for collaborative work because it has open seating, a more active environment, and good access to outlets.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours:
    • Daily: 8 AM to 10 PM

3. Klarman Hall

Klarman Hall has flexible open seating that can work well for meeting classmates and talking through assignments together.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Seating: Open collaborative seating
  • Hours: 24/7

Late-Night

1. Cocktail Lounge

Cocktail Lounge is one of the most reliable late-night study spaces for students who need a consistent place to work after evening classes.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours: 24/7

2. The Greenhouse Cafe & Cocktail Lounge

The Greenhouse Café & Cocktail Lounge is a flexible late-night option for students who prefer a more relaxed, social environment while working. It's especially useful when traditional study spaces feel too quiet or closed.

  • Noise: Low to Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available
  • Hours:
    • Monday–Friday: 12 PM to 11 PM
    • Saturday–Sunday: 12 PM to 12 AM

3. Goldwin Smith Hall Classrooms/Atriums

Feel free to enter any empty classroom or what is likely going to be an empty atrium for a completely peaceful late-night study session.

  • Noise: Medium
  • WiFi: Available
  • Outlets: Available in some areas
  • Hours: 24/7
